FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Seen With Care.

How can I start a chapter?

Visit our Chapters page to learn about the application process. We provide starter kits and support for new chapter leaders.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes! All donations are tax-deductible. They will appear on your statement under Hack+ (our fiscal sponsor).

What goes in a care bag?

Each bag includes socks, hygiene items, snacks, water, and a stamped postcard. See our full list on the Care Bags page.

How can I volunteer?

Check out our Get Involved page or contact us directly. We welcome volunteers of all ages!

Can I donate supplies instead of money?

Absolutely! Contact us for our current needs list and mailing address for supply donations.

Where do you operate?

We have 20 global chapters led by student changemakers. Find locations on our Chapters page.
