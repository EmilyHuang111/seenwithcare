Becoming a Chapter Head

Are you interested in changing the world? Do you view homelessness as an immense societal challenge that has yet to be solved?

Join Us as a Seen With Care Chapter Head!

As a Chapter Head, you will create and distribute bags, host events, fundraise, and make a difference in your community.

The best part? We'll fund your first round of bags!

A Seen With Care chapter is the perfect opportunity to apply ideas, strategies, and lessons to combat homelessness in your own city. Homelessness is a worldwide issue and deserves to be acknowledged and addressed in every corner of the globe.

Starting a Club at Your School

Get your school community involved and gain leadership experience.

If our mission resonates with you, we encourage you to start a club at your school! This is a great opportunity for you to get your community involved and expand your reach. Additionally, this is a great way for you to get leadership experience.

If you are interested, the first step is to reach out to a teacher at your school who might be interested in sponsoring your club. Once you have someone who is interested, reach out to the school club coordinator to officially begin meeting as a club.

Then, it's time to advertise! The beauty of Seen With Care is that there is no commitment necessary, so anyone can come whenever to help create a bag. Throughout the year, brainstorm fundraising opportunities to continue your amazing work, and certainly take advantage of any funds your school offers for club leaders.
