Why Join Our Team?

Seen With Care is a youth-led organization aimed at destigmatizing homelessness through direct aid and conversation. We've created and handed out 5,000+ care bags, raised over $5,000, and established chapters in 20 locations around the world.

Be Part of a Real Movement

Your work will directly impact people living outside all over the world.

Connect with Changemakers

Join a team that supports you in every aspect—from Seen With Care to school and beyond.

Learn Hands-On

Gain relevant experience plus support for future interviews and internships.

Grow Your Network

Make professional connections and meet potential mentors, speakers, and partners.

Use Your Voice

We want your ideas! Take initiative, start new projects, and help shape Seen With Care.

Get Recognized

Open Positions

Director of Marketing

Maintain an active & engaging social media presence. Create 2-4 Instagram posts a week plus regular stories. Craft LinkedIn posts for organizational updates.

4-5 hrs/week Graphic design preferred

Director of Outreach & Communications

Craft & send monthly newsletters. Reach out to potential speakers, sponsors, and partners. Conduct cold outreach to recruit new chapter directors.

3-4 hrs/week

Director of Operations & Technology

Oversee the website for accuracy and accessibility. Create important documents and resources. Organize data to track Seen With Care's impact.

3-4 hrs/week Tech skills preferred

Editorial Manager

Craft two articles/blog posts a month. Collaborate with the editorial team on educational content. Recruit guest writers and team members.

4-5 hrs/week Writing experience required
