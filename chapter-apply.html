What is Seen With Care?

Mission: Our mission is twofold: to humanize people living outside through direct aid and conversation and to destigmatize homelessness among youth and teens.

At Seen With Care, we create care bags full of everyday necessities for those living outside. These bags include items like food, water, warm gear, hygiene products, and so much more. Through our creation and direct distribution of these bags, we aim to spread awareness about the significant stigma that surrounds homelessness and lessen it through education and action.

What is a Seen With Care Chapter?

A Seen With Care chapter is the perfect opportunity to apply ideas, strategies, and lessons from Seen With Care to combat homelessness in your own city. Homelessness is a worldwide issue and deserves to be acknowledged and addressed in every corner of the globe.

Leading a Seen With Care chapter entails holding events, creating and delivering care bags, raising money, and spreading awareness in your own city.

Your Responsibilities

As a Chapter Head of Seen With Care, it is your role to make Seen With Care come to life in your community! This can include creating and distributing care bags, hosting events, reaching out to local shelters, starting a club at your school, and overall taking inspiration from Seen With Care to combat and destigmatize homelessness wherever you are.

Time Commitment 4-6 hours/week (flexible depending on your needs)
Recognition Eligible for Presidential Volunteer Service Award
