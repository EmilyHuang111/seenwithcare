Chapter Head

Nadia Foster

Nadia grew up taking the subway and seeing homelessness firsthand, which sparked her desire to make a difference. She's a member of her school's Model United Nations team and serves as editor of the school newspaper. Nadia is an avid reader who devours at least two books a week, enjoys urban photography, and spends weekends exploring different neighborhoods with her film camera. She's also part of a youth advocacy group focused on affordable housing in NYC.
