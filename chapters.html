Our Chapters

Making a difference across the world, one community at a time.

Seen With Care currently has 20 global chapters led by high-school changemakers. To create lasting change, we hope to reach individuals on every corner of the globe. Thus, our chapter program empowers young, global change-makers to adapt our idea in their own respective communities. To help them get started, each chapter receives an initial grant with all the supplies they need to host their first care bag distribution event. From there, their impact flourishes, and together, we make a collective difference.
