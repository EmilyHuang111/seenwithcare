What Drives Us

Our Mission & Purpose

We believe that those experiencing homelessness deserve not just help, but genuine connection and respect.

What We Do

We create care bags filled with everyday essentials and small luxuries requested by recipients themselves. From warm socks and hygiene kits to handwritten notes of encouragement.

Why We Do It

Our mission is to destigmatize homelessness. Those who live outside are worthy of dignity, respect, love, and care. Distributing a care bag acknowledges someone's humanity.

How We Do It

We support people living outside through direct aid. Our bags include socks, hats, gloves, food, water, hygiene kits, and stamped postcards. We provide resources for your community.
