Meet the Founder

Emily Huang

Emily Huang is a senior in high school and the founder of Seen With Care. Her mission was born from a connection she developed with a unhounsed man named Luis while walking to the school bus stop every day.

Under Emily's leadership, Seen With Care has impacted over 6,000 lives by reframing distribution as a platform for acknowledgment and conversation. Rather than simply handing out supplies, Emily has built an organization that prioritizes human connection and destigmatizes homelessness through meaningful interaction.

Beyond Seen With Care, Emily plays competitive tennis, codes apps, and volunteers in various other organizations, embodying a commitment to making a difference both through her organization and in her broader community.
